FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Here are the answers to the most common ones we receive.

If our technicians are unable to repair your device, you pay absolutely nothing — not even a diagnostic fee. You only pay if the repair is successful and your device is working. It's that simple, and it applies to every repair we undertake.

Most common repairs like screen replacements, battery swaps, and charging port fixes are completed same day — often in 30–60 minutes. More complex repairs such as logic board work or data recovery may take 1–3 business days. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you drop in or call.

Your data safety is our top priority. For most hardware repairs, your data remains completely untouched. If a repair requires a software wipe, we will always notify you in advance and advise you to back up your data first. We can also help with data backups if needed.

We use high-quality replacement parts that meet or exceed OEM specifications. For iPhones and MacBooks, we use premium aftermarket parts that deliver original performance and longevity. If you specifically require OEM parts, we can source those too — just ask when you book and we'll advise on pricing.

If your device is still under Apple warranty or AppleCare+, we recommend checking with Apple first as third-party repairs can sometimes affect manufacturer warranty coverage. However, if your device is out of warranty, which applies to most customers we see, it makes no difference and our repair is the best value option available.

We are based in Worcester Park and serve customers across London and Surrey. This includes Kingston, Wimbledon, Sutton, Croydon, Epsom, New Malden, Surbiton, Richmond, Twickenham, Putney, and many more areas. We also offer a collect and return service — call us to check if your area is covered.

Our diagnostics are completely free. Bring your device in, and we'll run a thorough check and tell you exactly what's wrong, along with a transparent quote. There's no pressure to proceed, and no charge if you decide not to go ahead with the repair.

Yes, absolutely. Every repair comes with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labour. If something we repaired fails within 90 days due to our workmanship or the parts we fitted, we'll repair it again at no cost to you. Our guarantee is our commitment to quality.
