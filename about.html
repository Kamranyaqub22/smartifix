Our Story

From a Passion for Tech to Thousands of Repairs

Smart iFix started in Worcester Park in 2020 with a single workbench and a simple idea: tech repairs should be honest, fast and affordable. Too many customers were being charged hundreds for repairs that cost a fraction — or told their device was unrepairable when it clearly was not.

Our founder, a certified Apple and PC technician with over 10 years of experience, set out to do things differently. Transparent pricing. No Fix No Fee. Plain English explanations. Repairs you can trust.

Today, Smart iFix has grown to a team of specialist engineers handling hundreds of repairs every month — MacBooks, laptops, iPhones, custom PCs, gaming consoles — all from our fully equipped workshop in Worcester Park.

Our Core Values

If we cannot repair your device you pay nothing. Not for the diagnostic, not for our time. Zero risk, always.

💬

Radical Transparency

Plain English explanations. Clear upfront quotes before work starts. You always know what is wrong and what it costs.

⚡

Speed & Urgency

We know you need your device. Most repairs are same day. We do not keep you waiting for weeks.

✅

Quality Parts Only

We use OEM-grade parts on every repair. No cheap knock-offs. Your device works perfectly after every repair.

Data Privacy

Your data is yours. We never access personal files without consent. All repairs done with complete privacy respect.
