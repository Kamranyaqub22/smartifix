iPhone Repair FAQs

Most iPhone screen replacements take between 30 and 60 minutes. We stock screens for the most popular iPhone models so you can often wait in-store while we complete the repair. Call ahead to confirm we have your specific model screen in stock.

On iPhone X through to iPhone 14 series, Face ID is tied to the original display's TrueDepth camera system, not the screen itself. A standard screen replacement does not affect Face ID provided the Face ID component is not damaged. We take great care during screen replacements to preserve your Face ID functionality.

We use high-quality aftermarket replacement screens that deliver performance equivalent to the original. These are premium parts that offer accurate colours, full touch sensitivity, and proper brightness. If you specifically require OEM Apple parts, please ask and we can advise on availability and pricing.

Don't turn it on, don't charge it, and don't put it in rice. Bring it to us as soon as possible. The sooner we can open the device and begin cleaning, the better the chance of a full recovery. We've successfully recovered many iPhones that owners thought were completely gone.

Yes, completely. Hardware repairs like screen and battery replacements don't affect your data at all. Your photos, apps, contacts and settings will be exactly as you left them. We only recommend a backup before a repair if there's a risk of a software reset being necessary, and we'll always tell you in advance.
