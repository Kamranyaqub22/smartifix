Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Smart iFix ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website (smartifix.co.uk), contact us, or have your device repaired by us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact details: name, phone number, email address
  • Device information: device make, model, and fault description you provide
  • Location: postcode or area you tell us about
  • Communication records: messages sent via our contact form, WhatsApp, or email
  • Usage data: anonymised website analytics (pages visited, time on site) via Google Analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide repair quotations and complete repair services
  • Communicate about your repair status
  • Process warranty claims
  • Respond to enquiries and support requests
  • Improve our website and services (via anonymised analytics)
  • Send service updates (only where you have consented)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following lawful bases (UK GDPR):

  • Performance of a contract: to provide repair services you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: to respond to enquiries and improve our business
  • Consent: where you have opted in to receive communications from us

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Formspree: Our contact form processor (formspree.io) — see their privacy policy
  • Google Analytics: Anonymised website usage data only
  • WhatsApp / Meta: When you contact us via WhatsApp
  • Legal authorities: Only where required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain customer repair records for up to 2 years for warranty purposes. Contact enquiry data is kept for up to 12 months. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@smartifix.co.uk.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please contact us immediately if you believe your data has been compromised.

Our No Fix No Fee guarantee means: if we cannot repair your device for any reason, you will not be charged for the repair or diagnostic. If you choose not to proceed with a quoted repair after a completed diagnostic, there is no charge.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to understand usage patterns. For information on Google's data practices, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored

  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all third-party cookies
